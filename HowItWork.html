Answers to questions

  • What is the check mark next to the profile of the instructor?
  • We mark the instructors who passed the examination
    of the documents and the certificate of the instructor on our site.
  • The instructor immediately receives payment for classes?
  • No, your money is in our safe until you confirm the work and its quality.
  • What to do if the work of the instructor is performed poorly?
  • After the end of the lesson, you will receive a notice in which you will be able
    to indicate that the work was performed not qualitatively, after which
    our specialists will contact you and the
    instructor to resolve the disputed situation.
  • Why do not I see the number of the instructor in the profile?
  • You will be able to contact the instructor after confirming the order.
